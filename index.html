Study on Trust by Integrating ChatGPT to Enhance Human-Robot Interaction (Fuhrat Robot)

[Kotlin | NLP | GPT | Hyperparameters]

The AI-Powered Trip Personal Assistant, named the "Atlas robot," focuses on integrating ChatGPT3 to enhance the user experience in trip planning. Key areas covered include Natural Language Processing (NLP) for voice commands, speech and voice recognition, and intent recognition. The project incorporates a recommendation system to offer personalized suggestions, catering to individual preferences. The overarching goal is to revolutionize the way users engage with technology during their journeys, providing a seamless and personalized travel assistant.

Machine Translation with Deep Neural Networks

[Kotlin | NLP | GPT | Hyperparameters]

Implemented a machine translation system from English to French using Deep Neural Networks and Keras. Executed data pre-processing raw text data by tokenizing, padding, lowercasing, and splitting validation and test sets to facilitate training and improve translation accuracy. Used and trained an Encoder-Decoder model using GRU cells with two layers for both the encoder and the decoder. This model incorporates embedding, bidirectional RNNs, and time-distributed dense layers for predicting each word in the output sequence. Trained and evaluated an alternative model with a similar architecture but using LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) cells instead of GRU cells.

Financial News Headline Sentiment Analysis

[Kotlin | NLP | GPT | Hyperparameters]

This project involves Sentiment Analysis of Financial News using the Natural Language Toolkit (NLTK). The goal is to predict sentiment in financial news, and the dataset includes three CSV files containing headlines from CNBC, The Guardian, and Reuters. NLTK, a platform for Python, is employed for text processing tasks such as tokenization, parsing, classification, stemming, tagging, and semantic reasoning. Sentiment analysis, the process of identifying positive or negative sentiment in text, is crucial for businesses to assess social data, manage brand reputation, and comprehend customer sentiments.
